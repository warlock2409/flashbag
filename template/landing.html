Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before you ship.

How do catalogs work?

Create items as goods, rentals, or services. Each type has its own attributes—stock, calendars, or schedules—yet lives in one unified catalog.

Which payments are supported?

Cards, wallets, bank debits, and invoicing in 135+ currencies. Taxes and compliance included.

Can I integrate my systems?

Yes—use webhooks and a full API for inventory, bookings, and orders. SDKs for web and mobile.

Is my data secure?

We apply encryption in transit and at rest with granular permissions and audit logs.
